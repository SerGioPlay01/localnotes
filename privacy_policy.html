Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 22, 2025

1. Introduction
This Privacy Policy describes how Local Notes ("the App"), operated by SerGio Play, handles information when you use the application available at https://localnotes-three.vercel.app/. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy applies to all users, including residents of California and other US states.

2. No Personal Data Collected
Local Notes does not collect, store, transmit, or process any personal information on our servers. The App has no backend server, no user registration, and no account system. All notes and app data are stored exclusively in your browser using LocalStorage and/or IndexedDB, encrypted with AES-256 encryption. Your data never leaves your device.

4. Google Analytics
The App uses Google Analytics (via gtag.js) to collect anonymized, aggregated usage statistics such as page views, session duration, and general geographic region. This data is used solely to understand how the App is used and to improve it. Google Analytics does not receive any note content or personally identifiable information. IP addresses are anonymized. Google may process this data in accordance with its own privacy policy available at https://policies.google.com/privacy. You may opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on at https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout.

5. Cookies
The App uses only cookies set by Google Analytics for analytics purposes. No other cookies are used. See our Cookie Policy for details.

6. Do Not Track
We respect Do Not Track (DNT) signals. If your browser sends a DNT signal, Google Analytics tracking will be limited to the extent technically feasible. The App itself does not track users beyond Google Analytics.

7. California Residents — CCPA Rights
Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), California residents have the following rights:
— Right to Know: You have the right to know what personal information we collect. We collect no personal information beyond anonymized analytics data.
— Right to Delete: You may request deletion of any personal information we hold. Since we hold none, there is nothing to delete on our end. You can delete all local app data by clearing your browser storage.
— Right to Opt-Out of Sale: We do not sell personal information. We have not sold personal information in the preceding 12 months.
— Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your CCPA rights.
To exercise your rights, contact us at sklarovs441@gmail.com.

8. Children's Privacy
The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us at sklarovs441@gmail.com.

9. Data Security
All notes stored by the App are encrypted using AES-256 encryption in your browser. Since no data is transmitted to our servers, there is no risk of server-side data breach affecting your notes.

10. Third-Party Services
The only third-party service used is Google Analytics. We do not share data with any other third parties. The App is hosted on Vercel; Vercel may collect standard server access logs (IP addresses, request timestamps) as part of hosting infrastructure, governed by Vercel's privacy policy.

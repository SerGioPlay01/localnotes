Origin Story

Why Local Notes was built

It started with a simple frustration — finding a notes app that was truly private, fast, and free.

The problem with existing apps
☁️
Everything syncs to a server. Notion, Evernote, Google Keep — your notes live on someone else's computer. One breach and your data is exposed.
💳
Encryption costs money. Most apps lock real encryption behind a paid plan. Privacy shouldn't be a premium feature.
📋
Registration walls everywhere. Just to write a note you need an email, a password, and to agree to a privacy policy that lets them use your data.
🌐
No offline support. Most web-based apps are useless without internet. Your notes should be available anywhere, always.
The search begins
Looking for a private notes app
Tried dozens of apps. Every option either required an account, synced to a cloud, lacked encryption, or charged for basic features. Nothing felt truly private.
The realization
The browser already has everything needed
Modern browsers have IndexedDB for local storage, the Web Crypto API for AES-256 encryption, and Service Workers for offline support. No server needed.
First prototype
Built a minimal working version
A simple editor, local storage, basic encryption. No frameworks, no dependencies. Just HTML, CSS, and vanilla JavaScript. It worked — and it was fast.
Growing the idea
Tags, calendar, 12 languages, PWA
Added rich text editing, color tags, a built-in calendar, transliteration search, and full localization for 12 languages. Packaged as a PWA for native-like installation.
Today — v1.1.0
Open source & free for everyone
Local Notes is MIT-licensed and free forever. The goal is simple: give everyone a secure, private place to write — without asking for anything in return.
🔒
The answer: keep everything in the browser
Local Notes uses your browser as a secure vault. AES-256 encryption, IndexedDB storage, Service Worker for offline — all built into modern browsers. No server, no account, no compromise.

Local Notes was built from the ground up with your privacy in mind. There is no server, no cloud sync, no analytics — just your browser and your encrypted data.

🛡️

👁️
Minimal tracking

🔑
No registration
Open the app and start writing. No email, no password, no account — ever.

AES-256 encryption
Industry-standard encryption protects your notes at rest. Only you can decrypt them.

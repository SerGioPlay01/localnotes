Cookie Policy

Latest update: January 22, 2025

We use cookies to ensure that the Local Notes web application works properly and to improve the user experience.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us to remember your settings and preferences and to ensure that the application functions reliably.

How do we use cookies?

Our web application uses cookies solely for:

1. Saving notes to LocalStorage - your data is only stored on your device and is not transferred to third-party servers.
2. Interface customization - to save your selected design themes and other user settings.
3. Application functionality - for correct operation of such functions as creating, editing, and deleting notes.

What data do we collect?

We do not collect or share users' personal data with third-party resources. All information, including notes created, is stored locally on your device and is only accessible to you.

Managing cookies

You can manage cookies in your browser settings, including deleting or blocking them. However, disabling cookies may affect the functionality of the Local Notes app, in particular it may cause you to lose your saved notes.

Policy changes

We reserve the right to make changes to this cookie policy. Updates will be posted on this page.

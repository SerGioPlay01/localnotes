Local Notes Beta Version

🚀 Beta Version

Local Notes is currently in beta. This means the application is fully functional and available to everyone, but we are still actively developing it, fixing bugs, and adding new features. Your experience and feedback are extremely valuable to us at this stage.

What does "beta" mean?

  • The app is stable and safe to use for your everyday notes.
  • Some features may change, be improved, or be added over time.
  • You may occasionally encounter minor bugs or rough edges — we appreciate your patience.
  • All your data stays on your device and is never sent to our servers.

What we are working on

  • Performance improvements and faster load times.
  • Better mobile experience and PWA support.
  • More export and import options.
  • Extended tag and calendar functionality.
  • Accessibility and localization improvements.

How to help If you find a bug or have a suggestion, please reach out to us. Your feedback directly shapes the future of Local Notes. You can contact the developer at sergioplay-dev.vercel.app.

Thank you Thank you for being an early user of Local Notes. You are helping us build something better for everyone.

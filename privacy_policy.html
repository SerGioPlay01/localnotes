Local Notes Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 22, 2025

1. Introduction This Privacy Policy describes how Local Notes ("the App"), operated by SerGio Play, handles information when you use the application. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

2. No Personal Data Collected Local Notes does not collect, store, transmit, or process any personal information on our servers. The App has no backend server, no user registration, and no account system. All notes are stored exclusively in your browser using LocalStorage and/or IndexedDB, encrypted with AES-256. Your data never leaves your device.

3. Data Controller Developer: SerGio Play
Contact: sklarovs441@gmail.com

4. Google Analytics The App uses Google Analytics (via gtag.js) to collect anonymized, aggregated usage statistics such as page views, session duration, and general geographic region. Google Analytics does not receive any note content or personally identifiable information. IP addresses are anonymized. You may opt out by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Add-on.

5. Cookies The App uses only cookies set by Google Analytics. No other cookies are used. See our Cookie Policy for details.

6. Do Not Track We respect Do Not Track (DNT) signals. If your browser sends a DNT signal, Google Analytics tracking will be limited to the extent technically feasible.

7. California Residents — CCPA Rights Under the CCPA, California residents have the right to know, delete, and opt-out of sale of personal information. We collect no personal information beyond anonymized analytics data and do not sell personal information. To exercise your rights, contact us at sklarovs441@gmail.com.

8. Children's Privacy The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Data Security All notes stored by the App are encrypted using AES-256 in your browser. Since no data is transmitted to our servers, there is no risk of server-side data breach affecting your notes.

10. Third-Party Services The only third-party service used is Google Analytics. The App is hosted on Vercel; Vercel may collect standard server access logs governed by their own privacy policy.

11. Offline and Online Modes In offline mode, no data is transmitted to any server. In online mode, Google Analytics may collect anonymized usage statistics. You can switch modes using the network toggle in the App footer.

12. Changes to This Policy We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

13. Contact For any privacy-related questions, contact us at: sklarovs441@gmail.com
